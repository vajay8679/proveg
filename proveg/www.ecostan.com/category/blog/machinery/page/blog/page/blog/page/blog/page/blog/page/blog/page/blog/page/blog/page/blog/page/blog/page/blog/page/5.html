A Pellet Machine is also known as ‘Pellet Press’ or ‘Pellet Mill.’ It is a machine or mill that integrates small materials into a giant homogeneous mass rather than breaking large items into tiny pieces. In other words, it makes pellets from the ground materials. Ecostan® highly durable pellet machines have a Precise operation, high performance, simple operability, less maintenance. We use excellent quality materials for making Pellets. The production capacity of our Pellet Making Machine scales from 700 to 900 Kilogram per hour (i.e., 8 millimeters to 10 millimeters in diameter).

Ecostan® has two types of Pellet Machines:

One is Flat Die Pellet Machine that has natural, suitable and multi-way pressure for all different material. And the other one is Ring Die Pellet Machine that offers features like low power consumption and high output in the feed grinding.

Ecostan® Cattle Feed Pellet Machines: This machine makes pellets of cattle feed which depends on the size required to make it easy to eat for the cattle. Raw material like rice husk, wheat stalk, bean pulp, straw, grass, and Corn, etc. are used.

Ecostan® Wood Pellet Machines: A wood pelletizing machine makes pellets from the materials like wood, straw, bamboo, cotton stalk, peanut shell, rice husk, waste paper, empty fruit bunch, and crushed coal, etc.

We offer supreme quality Pellet Machines that are mainly designed using the cutting edge technology. If you want to clarify anything about our products, call us now, We are ready to help you anytime.

How Much Is Saw Dust Needed To Make Finest Pellets?

Typically, for making pellets, a small saw dust is used. Initially, water is added to the sawdust until the moisture content of the raw material is between 8 to 15 percent which is regarded as the excellent moisture content for abrasion. But if you are using wood chips as a raw material, they should be pulverized first. And then put sawdust into the pellet machine slowly.
