Here are the tips on using Chipper Grinder and maintain the safety aspects

1. Wear Safety Gear

Chipper Grinder is use for recycling the waste and additional industrial material. In some of the procedure, the material comes out like dust from the machine that can be harmful to your eyes and body. So it is better using safety gear. Keeping eyes and ear protective are essential while using safety equipment. There are exclusive closed toe shoes available, which are recommendable while using the machine for large and heavy branches.

2.Choose the Chip Material Carefully

Chipper Grinder is designed to chip trees limbs and branches. They cannot be used for plastic, metal and processed wood. Make sure you only chip branches not non-bio material in Chipper Grinder.

3. Be careful while putting material for chipping

If you are putting the branch into the hopper, and it is not adjusting properly, do not try to push it with hands. It can damage your hands as well as create issues for the machine. It is better using the material of the required size and put with safe hands.

4. Keep Kids and Pets Away From Running Machine

Kids and pets do not know the functionality of the machine. So it is better placing the Chipper Grinder away from the reach of them.

Rotary Drum Dryer




Rotary Drum Dryer is also known as rotary kiln at some places that have been designed to make forest and agro waste reusable by maintaining moisture in the material. The whole procedure is based on the heating of material and removes the gas that has been developed during the process. The machine has been made up of rotating and large cylindrical tube. The machine dryer discharge end is lower than the material feed.

The material Rotary Drum Dryers processes:

Rotary Drum Dryers is the best replacement of industrial dryer as they process a wide variety of material. This is one of the best industrial drying solutions. Some of the tools are designed to process a particular type of material whereas some are for multi-type material. Rotary Drum Dryers processes animal feeds, animal waste, product waste, coconut shell, paddy husk, municipal sludge, concentrates as well as municipal waste.

Working process of Rotary Drum Dryer

The mechanism is based on the use of hot air. The hot air generated by Briquettes and wood. The user needs to screw the material at feeding points. The material is spread by lifters in the horizontal shell. Furthermore, hot air is sucked into the internal process for wet material. The cylinder rotates downwards for material flow down, at the end point material comes out with rotary valve and cyclone with required moisture. While material comes out, the evaporated moisture goes into the atmosphere with the help of a fan.
